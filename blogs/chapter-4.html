Coffee

Post on Thu, 26 Oct 2023

The best algorithm to make coffee.

 I have always thought that I'm a coffee lover, I do have the habit where I have to drink coffee daily especially morning, afternoon and evening (optional). But, I have this realization that I didn't really love coffee, the only thing that I really love is Nescafe.

 Don't get me wrong, I still drink any other type of coffee be it black, latte, americano etc (I didn't really know what's the different between them tho, and I always have to asked for my wife opinion whenever I want to order at Starbucks or something.) My safe choice will always be vanilla latte as it is the only coffee that I think will never go wrong? I guess. All I wanted to say is I can drink coffee and I like all of them but, Nescafe is the only one that I can drink daily.

 How I like my coffee? I like it with sugar and a little bit sweet, I don't like my coffee too bitter, a little bitter is just nice. The way I prepare my coffee is:

  1. I usually wash the spoon and my mug first, this is because I don't know who's the last to use them and if they really wash it cleanly ? I don't like my mug smell like a food (You know when someone did't wash it properly and the mug kinda smell like the last thing they eat? Fish is the worst, can you imagine coffee + fish ?).
  2. I don't really know the type of spoon, I really called them all spoon no matter what the type. So usually I use one and a half scoop of sugar using whatever spoon in front of me or the one that easy to reach and then put in into the mug.
  3. For the Nescafe, I put only a little maybe just 1/5 of the spoon. Yes I really use a small amount of nescafe so that it don't taste too bitter.
  4. For the water, I use hot water from water filter that that have those hot and warm water pipe with them (the one that you have to pay monthly for servicing etc). I put it maybe 4/5 of the mug, not too full because I don't want it to spill when I stir it.
  5. I didn't drink it right away, I don't like very hot coffee, I will put it somewhere high (where my daughter can't reach) and wait 5-10 minutes before drinking it.

 This is how I prepare and drink my Nescafe everyday, this is the best coffee for me and you can try it if you want. This is open source How To Make Coffee algorithm that work best for me.

 Well, sorry if I confuse you by using the term coffee and Nescafe all over the place, they are the same for me and if I said coffee, it just Nescafe really.

 Enjoy you coffee or Nescafe or whatever.
